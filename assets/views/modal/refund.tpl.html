RFID Manual Refund

Pentru recuperarea banilor ce au fost incarcati la fata locului trebuie sa urmezi pasi de mai jos:

1. Trebuie sa ai cont pe ec2016.pay.intellifest.com
2. Daca nu ai cont, creeaza-ti unul si inregistreaza-ti bratara folosind codurile unice de pe spate.
3. Du-te la Refund
4. Completeaza datele in formular pana in 31 Iulie.
5. Banca va procesa plata, iar banii tai vor fi transferati in contul tau.

Fiecare operatiune este supusa unei taxe de 3 lei ce reprezinta taxele bancare de transfer manual.

In order to refund the money that were deposited on you RFID card at the festival, please folow the next steps:

1. You need an account on ec2016.pay.intellifest.com
2. If you don't have one, create it and register your bracelet tag with the code you will find on it's back.
3. Go to Refund
4. Fill the required data until July 31.
5. The bank will process your request and the money will be transferred into your account.

Each operation will be charged with 3 lei representing the manual transfer bank tax.
