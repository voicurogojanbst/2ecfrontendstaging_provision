Camping zones

Camping zones

Where is the camping situated and what can I find inside?

This year there is only one camping area, situated in the same place where the Premium Camping was last year, but with much more facilities – premium toilets and showers, free lockers, LIDL shop, grill zone, coffee lounge, laundromat, customer service point. It gives access to different camping add-ons (Pre-pitched tents, Dream Tents, Swedish Lodges and Panorama Tents).

What are the camping add-ons?

If you don’t want to bring your tent, you can choose from Pre-pitched tents, Dream Tents, Swedish Lodges and Panorama Tents. Details here.

How much they cost?

You can find all the information you need regarding the costs here.

When will the camping area be open?

From 12th of July – 14:00 until 17th of July – 14:00, 24 hours a day.

Can I enter with a car in the camping area?

No. It is prohibited to enter the camping area or its access point with any vehicle.

Will there be security?

Yes. The entire perimeter of the camp will be supervised by security guards. However, we recommend you not to leave any valuables in your tent.

Can I bring food?

Yes, you can.

Can I make fire?

No, you can’t

Can I bring alcohol?

No, you can’t.

Can I bring pets?

Sorry, but no.

Can bring an electricity generator?

Electricity generators, stoves and barbecues are prohibited in the camping area.

RV Camping

RV Camping

How can I come with my caravan?

Firstly, you need to buy a ticket from electriccastle.ro/tickets. Each caravan must have an RV ticket.

Can I park my car in the RV Area?

No. Only cars with fixed beds can park there.

Will I be connected to electricity?

Yes. Each RV will get 1kw power.

Do I have a water source?

There is a water spot in the RV camping which can be used by the campers in that area.

Can I have a side tent/terrace?

Yes, each RV may have one side tent/terrace.

If I have an RV ticket can I enter the camping areas?

No, to enter the camping, each person must have a General Access+Camping or a Premium Access + Camping.

 Transportation

Transportation

Is there a bus from Cluj-Napoca to Bontida?

Yes, there will be regular buses on the route Cluj-Bontida and Bontida – Cluj leaving from the Horia Demian sports hall parking and Iulius Mall.

What is the schedule?

24/7. The buses will leave from 20 to 20 minutes. The last bus to Cluj will leave on Monday, 13:00 PM

How can I buy bus tickets?

At each station, there will be a Ticket Booth where you can buy tickets. You can also pay with your RFID wristband.

Can I get there by car?

Yes, but you can’t enter Bontida without a valid car pass. All cars will be directed to the parking area.

Where do I park?

You can find our parking here.

What’s the price of the parking?

Each car will have to pay 30 RON/entrance. After you pay, you can leave your car there for the whole festival. If you leave the parking, once you return you will have to pay 30 RON again.

Can I come by bike?

Yes, you can, we have a bike parking lot near the festival entrance.

Can I come by train?

Yes, all the trains that pass Bontida will stop there, during the festival.
