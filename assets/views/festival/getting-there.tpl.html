Getting There

By Train

We recommend you to take the train to Gara Cluj-Napoca. To get to Electric Castle you can:

1. Take the train to Bontida - also during the festival any train that passes Bontida, will stop there. Checkout the schedule here
2. Take the tram 101 or 102 to Horia Demian Sports Hall or take the bus 9 to Memorandumului, then bus 25 to Iulius Mall. From these two locations, shuttle service will get you to the festival grounds.

By Plane

We recommend you to fly to Cluj-Napoca Airport (airportcluj.ro) (30 km from the festival site). From the airport, you can either take a transfer shuttle or a taxi that will get you to the festival site. For the return trip, you can use one of our buses to get to the airport.

For the transfer shuttle please make a reservation here:
transfero.ro/bilete-online.

By Bus

There will be regular buses, all day and night long, leaving from Horia Demian sports hall and Iulius Mall. Depending on the number of people waiting, the waiting time between buses may vary between 10 - 25 minutes.

The tickets can be bought from the bus stations or directly at the bus entrance using your festival wristband.

The last bus to Cluj will leave on Monday, 13:00 PM.

If you're coming from abroad you can use Flixbus to get to Cluj-Napoca. Details here: Flix Bus.
