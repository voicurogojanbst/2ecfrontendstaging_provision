How to buy tickets

  1. 1.
    Go to the tickets page.
    If you're from abroad, visit Festicket.
    If you’re from The Netherlands or Belgium, visit Festival.Travel.

    If you want to buy a hard-copy ticket you can get them from our partner locations.
    Go now
  2. 2.
    Choose your Pass.
    •  General Access
    •  General Access with Camping
    •  Premium Pass
    •  Premium Pass with Camping
    •  The Black Ticket
  3. 3.
    Enhance your camping experience with one of our awesome add-ons:
    •  Pre-Pitched Tents
    •  Dream Tents
    •  Swedish Lodges
    •  Panorama Tents
    See all
  4. 4. Click Finalize Order, then login or create your account.
  5. 5. Click Pay Now.
  6. 6. Make the payment by entering your bank card details.
  7. 7. Keep your confirmation email safe!
  8. 8.
    (Optional) Upgrade your ticket
    In order to upgrade you pass you need to login with our account and you will see the upgrade option in your orders section.
    Any ticket can be upgraded, for example from General Pass to General Pass with Camping or from General Pass to Premium Pass.
  9. 9.
    Receive/download your tickets(s)
    After the purchase, you will receive the order confirmation on your email. Keep that email!
    Also, on your account, on the Orders section you will see the purchased tickets and the individual codes for each.

    Starting mid-April you will be able to download your tickets as PDF files.

    If your ticket if a gift and need your ticket right now, write us an email at contact@electriccastle.ro and we'll help you out.
